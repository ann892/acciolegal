COVER / OPENING
================================================

Proposal for Kalolytic

At Accio Legal, we understand that for a high-growth company such as Kalolytic,
maintaining a clean, compliant, and strategically aligned equity structure is
critical for operational stability, founder control, and future fundraising.

Pursuant to our recent discussions, we have identified certain structural,
compliance, and transactional gaps in the Company's current shareholding
framework, which require immediate attention and execution.

This proposal outlines our end-to-end legal approach to:
- Regularize pending share allotments
- Facilitate exit of identified shareholders
- Optimize founder shareholding and control
- Align the capital structure with upcoming ESOP and fundraising plans

================================================
SCOPE OF SERVICES — MODULAR PHASE FORMAT
================================================

We propose to undertake a comprehensive equity restructuring and compliance
mandate, covering the following key areas:

---

PHASE 1: Regularization of Pending Share Allotment

Accio Legal will ensure that the pending allotment of shares (for which
consideration has already been received) is completed in compliance with
applicable laws.

Scope of Work:
- Review of transaction structure and applicable provisions under the
  Companies Act, 2013
- Structuring of allotment under private placement framework
- Drafting of:
  - Board Resolutions
  - Allotment documentation
  - Issuance of share certificates
- Filing of requisite forms (including PAS-3)

Outcome:
- Legally compliant and fully documented share allotment
- Elimination of regulatory exposure arising from delayed issuance

---

PHASE 2: Exit Structuring of Existing Shareholders

Accio Legal will facilitate the exit of identified shareholders in a legally
compliant and tax-efficient manner, while protecting the Company's interests.

A. Buyback of Shares (Subject to Eligibility)
- Structuring buyback under Section 68 of the Companies Act, 2013
- Assessment of:
  - Free reserves availability
  - Buyback limits (including 10% threshold for Board approval route)
- Drafting of:
  - Buyback offer documents
  - Board resolutions
- Regulatory compliance and filings

Strategic Impact:
- Increase in promoter shareholding
- Reduction in external shareholding without dilution

OR

B. Secondary Share Transfer (Alternative Mechanism)
- Structuring transfer to incoming investors / identified parties
- Drafting and execution of:
  - Share Transfer Deeds
  - Board approvals
- Advisory on:
  - Pricing structure (including split consideration models)
  - Capital gains tax implications

Strategic Impact:
- Facilitates smooth exit
- Optimizes tax exposure for stakeholders

---

PHASE 3: Founder Shareholding Optimization

Accio Legal will restore and maintain desired promoter control thresholds.

Scope of Work:
- Cap table analysis and restructuring strategy
- Advisory on target shareholding thresholds and dilution planning
- Structuring of Rights Issue (if required) and selective participation to
  rebalance holdings

Outcome:
- Strengthened promoter control
- Improved governance positioning for future investors

---

PHASE 4: Integration with Future Capital Raise

Accio Legal will ensure that the revised equity structure is investor-ready
and scalable.

Scope of Work:
- Alignment of cap table with proposed fundraising
- Advisory on valuation-linked issuance
- Drafting support for Rights Issue / further issuance and investor entry
  structuring

Outcome:
- Clean and diligence-ready equity structure
- Seamless transition into next funding round
